Operation

Customized development

Millennium Textiles highly experienced in house team of designers have the expertise to develope and customized collection for clients to promote their own brands. Our design studio and facilities for development of proto models, strike offs, bit loam swatches are there to experiment and create collections exclusively for clients. Costs of development are borne by Millennium Textiles when minimum of quantity and value are met. We work to establish a close and long term relationship.

Weaving

The Weaving unit has 67 shuttle-less Sulzer looms. The installation of these looms is supported by modern yarn preparation equipment, making them comparable with the finest quality yarn preparation equipment anywhere in the world. These shuttle-less Sulzer looms enable the production of a wide range of fabrics with high precision and efficiency, catering to diverse market demands. Moreover, this advanced technology minimizes waste and enhances productivity, ensuring sustainable and environmentally friendly operations. The integration of these looms with cutting-edge yarn preparation tools further solidifies the unit's commitment to delivering top-tier woven products.

Processing

Millennium Textiles boasts a cutting-edge processing unit to complement our expertise in customized development and weaving. Equipped with modern dyeing facilities, advanced finishing techniques, and stringent quality control measures, our processing unit ensures the production of high-quality textiles that meet the most exacting industry standards. Sustainability is a core focus, with eco-friendly practices integrated to minimize waste and energy consumption. Our commitment to efficiency, innovation, and environmental responsibility underscores our dedication to fostering enduring partnerships with clients seeking top-tier textile solutions.

Cutting

Cutting department comprises of experienced cutting masters, so that the cutting is as per requirement. We have a cutting department staff to carry out effective inspection.The inspectors carry out random examination of cutting operations so that fair representation of cutting production can be achieved.

Stitching

Stitching of textile made-ups is carried out with the aid of sophisticated machinery, hands-on expertise resulting in an exquisite finish. This combination of advanced technology and skilled craftsmanship ensures precision and quality in every stitch, enhancing the aesthetic appeal and durability of the products. The Stitching department's commitment to excellence is evident in their meticulous attention to detail, setting a high standard in the industry and ensuring customer satisfaction with every textile item produced.

Packing

Very special emphasis is given to packing at Millennium Textiles. In addition to standard packing, a variety of fancy packing options are available to fulfill customers' requirements. This focus on packaging ensures that products not only meet but exceed quality and presentation expectations, reflecting the company's dedication to delivering excellence in every aspect of its service. The adaptability in offering custom packing solutions demonstrates Millennium Textiles' commitment to customer satisfaction and attention to detail in every step of the process.

Bailing & Warehouse

The Purpose and scope of bailing section is to ensure that it meets the buyers standard.All rolls are made on the "A" frame are wrapped in Poly bags and sown in the poly propylene cloth. On the other hand the bails are made by high pressure bailing machines During the last few years Millennium Textiles has constructed a technologically advance warehouse with afloor space of 59000sqft covered area, helping customers to increase flexibility and the ability to serve the ever changing market requirements.
